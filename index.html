Projects

WEST AFRICAN COUNTRIES POLITICAL VIOLENCE TRENDS ANALYSIS FROM 1997-2025

This project examined political violence trends across West Africa from 1997 to 2025 using data from the Armed Conflict Location & Event Data Project (ACLED). The analysis focused on the frequency, intensity, and geographic distribution of conflict events and related fatalities. Using Power BI and Excel, I developed interactive dashboards to visualize event patterns, identify high-risk countries, and highlight key periods of escalation. The insights aimed to support policymakers and peacebuilding organizations in understanding regional conflict dynamics and promoting sustainable peace.

TECHSOLUTIONS GHANA IT OPTIMIZATION ANALYSIS

A data-driven analysis for TechSolutions Ghana aimed at improving client retention, project delivery, and service profitability. The project explored trends in retention, completion times, and revenue across IT services to identify inefficiencies and top-performing areas. Insights guided recommendations to boost loyalty, streamline processes, and focus on the most profitable services.

MEDIHEALTH GHANA LIMITED SALES ANALYSIS

Designed and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze MediHealth Ghana Limited's sales performance (2023–2024). Transformed transactional data into actionable insights on revenue, product performance, customer segments, and regional trends. Key metrics include GHS 270.16K total revenue and 98,280 units sold, with clear breakdowns by customer type, region, product, and time. Enabled strategic decision-making for inventory optimization, customer targeting, and market expansion.
